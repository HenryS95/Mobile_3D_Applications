Reflections

Having started this module with zero experience in any of the languages required and only a brief experience with C#, I am pleased with my work. However, as I’m sure you’re aware, not all the requirements of the mark scheme have been met. Notably, my site is not structured in an MVC format. Due to my inexperience, and the time it took to develop a new workflow for using Cinema4D, I was not able to achieve a functioning MVC structure in time. Finding relevant information about X3D’s functionality was particularly challenging. The X3DOM tutorial site was useful but it did not cater to my specifics requirements. This meant I spent a lot of time trying to join the dots between various sources. I hope that the work I have provided reflects the extent of time troubleshooting to achieve the other main requirements of this module.
